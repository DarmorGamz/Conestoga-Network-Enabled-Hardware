Introduction

Currently, there are not many viable options for monitoring your volume usage in realtime. All the available competitors are not non-invasive and require a plumber, or a special skill set to install. The available options currently in the market are also only available in an analog view; As a consumer you’d need to access the pipe you’re monitoring to take a reading for your volume usage. The undertaking of our project is to allow any skill level of consumer to install their self, and view there usage anywhere by visiting our webpage.

Goal

Our design is intended to allow a customer to install a flow reader non-invasively, and without paying for a plumber; as well as viewing their usage in real time.
